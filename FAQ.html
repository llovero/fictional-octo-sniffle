FAQ - Fly Miles

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes LLT-Racing different from other performance shops?
We specialize in Honda and Mitsubishi vehicles, offering tailored performance upgrades, OEM-spec engine rebuilds, and custom engine conversions. Our passion for these brands and attention to detail sets us apart.
Can you help with performance upgrades for track days?
Absolutely! Whether you're looking to fine-tune your car's suspension, enhance engine performance, or install a roll cage, we can prep your Honda or Mitsubishi for the track.
Do you only work on Hondas and Mitsubishis?
While our specialty is Honda and Mitsubishi vehicles, we’re happy to discuss custom projects for other makes and models. Reach out, and we’ll see how we can help!
Can you rebuild my engine to factory specs?
Yes, we offer full OEM-spec engine rebuilds to restore your car to its original glory while maintaining factory reliability and performance standards.
I want to swap a different engine into my car. Can you do that?
Engine conversions are our specialty! Whether it’s a B-series for your Honda or an Evo swap, we can handle the fabrication and installation needed to make it work flawlessly.
Do you offer dyno tuning?
Yes, we provide dyno tuning to ensure your car is performing at its peak. We use advanced tools to maximize horsepower and torque while keeping your car reliable.
How long does an engine rebuild or upgrade take?
The timeline depends on the complexity of the job. A basic rebuild might take 2-3 weeks, while custom conversions or performance builds can take longer. We’ll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation.
What’s the best way to get a quote?
You can call us, email us, or drop by our shop. Bring your car and any specific goals or issues you have in mind, and we’ll provide a personalized quote.
