Explore Our Services

We offer a range of services tailored to suit our customers' needs. Our work is backed by a warranty*—a testament to our confidence in the quality of what we deliver.Below are just some of the services we provide. Please feel free to inquire via email or through our "Contact Us" page. We aim to respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours.

We specialize in performance engine builds and OEM-spec rebuilds.

We specialize in building high-performance engines that deliver power, precision, and reliability. Whether you want a custom-built engine designed for maximum performance or a rebuild to restore your engine to OEM standards, we’ve got you covered. Whether you're gearing up for the track or bringing your engine back to its original glory, our skilled team ensures every project gets the attention it deserves, with exceptional results every time.

We specialize in engine conversions and fabrications for Mitsubishi and Honda vehicles.

At our workshop, we’re passionate about engine conversions and custom fabrications, focusing exclusively on Mitsubishi and Honda vehicles. Whether you’re dreaming of a powerful engine swap, boosting performance, or creating a setup that’s truly unique, we’re here to make it happen. Every project we take on is handled with care, precision, and a commitment to quality that ensures it’s done right the first time. From fine-tuning performance to crafting engines that feel like they just rolled out of the factory, we welcome all Mitsubishi and Honda enthusiasts looking to take their vehicles to the next level.

We specialize in boring, honing, decking, balancing, and custom head work.

We take pride in offering a full range of machine work to fine-tune your engine for performance or restore it to OEM specs. From boring, honing, and decking to detailed cylinder head rebuilding, we ensure every part is crafted with care. We also provide dynamic balancing for smooth performance, along with specialty services like port and polish (stage 1-4), valve angle jobs, and fitting oversized valves. Whatever your engine needs, we’re here to make sure it runs at its best.

We offer coilover, sway bar, bushing installations, and brake upgrades.

We’re here to help you get the most out of your ride with suspension upgrades like coilovers, sway bars, and bushing installations, all designed to improve handling and comfort. Plus, we offer front and rear brake upgrades, giving you the stopping power you need for a smooth and responsive driving experience. Whatever your driving needs, we’ve got the parts and expertise to make it happen.

Certified Car Repair Service Provider

We are a Certified Car Repair Service Provider committed to delivering top-notch automotive care. Our expert technicians are fully certified to handle a wide range of car repairs, from routine maintenance to complex engine diagnostics. With a proven track record of excellence, we’ve earned multiple industry awards for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.Experience the difference with a trusted service provider dedicated to your car’s performance and longevity.
